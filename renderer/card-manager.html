Card Manager Instructions
  • Use the filters above to narrow down cards by type, brand, model, or generation.
  • Card types include: Feature, Product Options, Specification Table, Weather Protection, Cargo Options.
  • Click a card to view or edit its details.
  • Only the cards currently shown (filtered) will be uploaded when you start a bulk upload.
  • You can also upload cards individually by using the upload button on each card.
  • The "Cancel Operation" button will appear during bulk uploads or imports. Click it to stop the current operation.
  • If you add new card types, update the filter dropdown and mapping in the code.
